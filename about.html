Our Story

Discover the passion behind WaveHaven

Welcome to WaveHaven

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate surfers, WaveHaven was born from a simple idea: to create the perfect home base for surfers exploring the incredible waves of Gran Canaria.

What started as a small beach house with a few bunk beds has grown into Las Palmas' premier surf hostel, combining affordable accommodation with top-notch surf services and a vibrant international community.

Our mission is to help you experience the best waves Gran Canaria has to offer while providing a comfortable, social, and authentic surf travel experience.

Our Values

What we stand for at WaveHaven

Ocean Respect

We're committed to protecting the beaches and oceans we love through regular cleanups and sustainable practices.

Community

We believe the best surf trips are about the people you meet. Our hostel fosters connections between guests from around the world.

Sustainability

From solar panels to water-saving measures, we minimize our environmental impact without compromising comfort.

Local Knowledge

As locals, we share our insider tips to help you experience Gran Canaria beyond the tourist spots.
